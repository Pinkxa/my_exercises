Hi! My name is Mzimkhulu Collin Duda, I've been in prison for 12 years for arm robbery. Sihle was already out and he opened NGO called Brothers For All, I then partnered with him, I am managing Craft side as this organisation is a combination of programming and craft. Our aim is to decrease unemployment rate looking at vulnerable communities, ex-offenders and dropouts. I'm a hardworker and passionate about my work and aspired to change people's lives to the community that I neglected before going to prison.
